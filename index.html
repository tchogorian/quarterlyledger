How the Ledger Works

A standing reference, revised every quarter.

I · Quarterly Issue

Published four times a year.

Each issue refreshes the full universe from the latest EDGAR filings. Picks are promoted, demoted, or retired based on fundamentals and situation progress.

II · Tear Sheets

One page per security.

Ten-year financial history, Munger-framework score, analyst summary, and "what a screen can't see" — the filing-level detail that doesn't surface in screeners.

III · Your Saves

Build your own watch.

Hit Save on any tear sheet. Your list lives in your browser — no account, no email, no tracking. Revisit on every issue to see what's moved.
