A note from the editor

Good research shouldn't cost twelve thousand dollars a year.

I · Why

Small-cap is where work still matters.

Large-caps are picked over by thousands of analysts. The bottom half of the public market is picked over by almost no one. That's where we look.

II · How

Filings first, always.

Every tearsheet is built from 10-Ks, 10-Qs, 8-Ks, proxies, and DEF 14As. No scraped data. No third-party summaries. If it's in the register, we read the filings.

III · What

A standing reference, revised quarterly.

Four issues a year. Names get promoted, demoted, or retired based on fundamentals and situation progress. The Ledger is a living register, not a one-time report.

IV · Yours

Your watchlist, locally.

Hit Save on any tearsheet. The list lives in your browser — never on our servers. No account, no email, no unsubscribe page.
