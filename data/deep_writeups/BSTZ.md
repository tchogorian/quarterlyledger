### ANALYST SUMMARY
BSTZ is a $1.7B closed-end fund holding listed and unlisted growth-tech equities, structured as a "term trust" scheduled to wind up in 2031. The fund pays an 8% managed distribution funded substantially from return of capital, and trades at $24.97 vs. NAV of ~$27.46 (9.1% discount). Saba Capital filed SC 13D/A on March 13, 2026 disclosing 7.94% beneficial ownership after accumulating ~5.46M shares (~$93.8M) Jan-March. Saba has already nominated trustees for the 2026 proxy season. The discount has persisted in part because the market discounts BlackRock's marks on illiquid privates such as Databricks and Stripe — and partly because rising rates strained CEF demand.

The bear case: NAV itself may be optimistic. BSTZ carries Databricks, Stripe, and other late-stage privates at quarterly fair-value marks, and a sector reset (or a Stripe down-round) would close the "discount" by lowering NAV rather than lifting price. Term-trust structure gives BlackRock until 2031 to engineer an exit, so there is no enforceable hard catalyst. Saba precedents at BIGZ/BMEZ produced sub-NAV (98%) tenders capped at 40-50% of shares — meaning the discount only partially monetizes, and residual NAV may re-discount post-tender. An 8% distribution that is partly destructive ROC also caps total return.

The bull case is settlement geometry: Saba's BIGZ playbook (52.5% tender at 99.5% NAV in 2024) is the obvious template, and BlackRock has shown willingness to settle to avoid messy contested votes. A 30-50% tender at 98-99% of NAV on a $24.97 stock with $27.46 NAV harvests roughly 10-13% on the tendered slice. Add private-IPO upside (Stripe, Databricks listing optionality 2026-27) and discount-narrowing on the residual. Annual meeting trustee vote is the gating event in Q2-Q3 2026.

### WHAT A SCREEN CAN'T SEE

Saba's accumulation pace (5.46M shares for $93.8M = ~$17.18 avg) suggests they bought in below current market, meaning their basis is well below NAV. Importantly, BSTZ has a pre-existing "Discount Management Program" that automatically triggers tender consideration if the average discount exceeds 7.5% — a board-approved governance mechanism Saba can lever in proxy materials. Watch for a settlement announcement before the 2026 record date.
