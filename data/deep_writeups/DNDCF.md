### THE SETUP
Goodman family Canadian mining holdco trades at a ~65% discount to indicative NAV (~$3.50/sh vs $1.20 price). $200M+ of crystallized cash from 2025-2026 monetizations (New Found Gold unit sale + Borborema royalty sale to Gold Royalty Corp Jan 2026) sits inside a $95M market cap.

### ANALYST SUMMARY
Dundee Corporation is a Toronto-listed family holding company controlled by the Goodman family. Founder Ned Goodman built and then nearly destroyed it through a series of failed mining and energy bets in the 2010s. Son Jonathan Goodman has been executing a pivot toward a "scalable mining platform" — monetizing legacy positions, returning capital, and positioning to attract third-party LP capital. The portfolio includes residual public mining stakes (New Found Gold, Reunion Gold), the Westhaven Gold Shovelnose JV/earn-in (~C$85M staged), private positions (United Hydrocarbon, AgriMarine), and the recently sold Borborema gold royalty (US$45M cash to Gold Royalty Corp, closed January 2026).

The bear case is the canonical Canadian small-cap holdco trap: tiny float (~$95M USD market cap), illiquid junior mining positions whose marks are speculative, multiple-voting shares that cement Goodman family control and prevent shareholder-led restructuring, and a long history of value destruction that justifies investor skepticism. Even with $200M+ of crystallized cash, the family may reinvest it into the "mining platform" rather than returning it. NAV-discount holdcos can stay discounted for decades — Loews, Pargesa, and many others have shown the pattern. Liquidity risk is real for any institutional position.

The bull case is mathematical: $200M of cash inside a $95M shell is a 2x asymmetric setup before any other asset moves. Jonathan Goodman has demonstrably executed multiple monetizations (this is not promise — it is realized cash). Catalysts include continued public-stake monetization, Westhaven Shovelnose milestone payments, possible Goodman-family take-private (the cleanest exit), large buyback or special dividend, and launch of a third-party-funded mining platform that would crystallize a management-fee multiple on the parent. Even at a permanent 50% discount, current price implies meaningful upside.

### WHAT A SCREEN CAN'T SEE
Dundee's MD&A discloses public-market investment positions at fair value, but the private positions (United Hydrocarbon Chad oil concession, AgriMarine, various junior mining warrants) are carried at cost or stale marks. The "Subordinate Voting Shares" structure — Class A subordinate vs Class B common with 100:1 voting — is detailed only in the AIF and prevents any non-Goodman-family change of control. The January 2026 8.6% press release noted the Borborema royalty sale closing; the proceeds are not yet visible in any consensus financial database.

### CATALYSTS
- Westhaven Shovelnose earn-in milestone payments (2026)
- Capital return announcement (special dividend / SIB / buyback)
- Possible Goodman-family take-private bid
