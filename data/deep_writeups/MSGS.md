### ANALYST SUMMARY
Madison Square Garden Sports Corp. is a single-asset stub that owns 100% of the New York Knicks (NBA) and 100% of the New York Rangers (NHL), plus their minor-league affiliates — the Hartford Wolf Pack (AHL) and Westchester Knicks (G League). Forbes and Sportico value the Knicks around $7.5B and the Rangers around $4.0B, implying an aggregate franchise value north of $11B before considering league media tailwinds. At a $322 share price and ~$8B market cap, the equity trades at roughly a 31% discount to indicative NAV — and that gap has been remarkably persistent due to the Dolan-family control overhang.

The bear case is structural. James Dolan controls the company via dual-class shares, and his public profile, history of public feuds, and reluctance to sell or distribute create a permanent governance discount. Sports franchises trade infrequently in public markets; Forbes valuations are appraisals, not realized prices. There is no dividend, and capital return has historically been minimal. NBA and NHL collective bargaining agreements introduce salary-cap and revenue-sharing changes that can compress per-team economics. MSG Networks is a separate, troubled RSN stub whose problems can spill back into perception of MSGS.

The bull case is finally activatable. In February 2026 the board unanimously approved exploring a possible spin-off or minority-stake sale of one of the franchises — the first concrete mark-to-market catalyst in MSGS's history. Recent private comps make denial untenable: the Boston Celtics sold at $6.1B in 2025, the Lakers at $10B in 2025, and the Senators at $1.3B. Citi and JPMorgan target north of $370 in early 2026; Susquehanna raised to $388 in April 2026. The new NBA media-rights deal flows through 2026–2027 and should re-base the team-level revenue assumption.

### WHAT A SCREEN CAN'T SEE
The 10-K contains the league revenue-sharing footnotes that determine how much of the new NBA media-rights deal actually flows to each franchise (and how much is shared) — that flow-through is the single biggest 2026–2027 earnings driver and is opaque to standard quant models. The proxy discloses the dual-class voting structure and Dolan-family supervoting shares; this is the discount and also the reason any sale must be Dolan-blessed. Watch the credit-agreement covenants in the 10-K exhibits — they constrain how much capital can be returned even if a minority sale closes. The MSG Networks reorganization filings (separate company) will mark RSN-related contingent recoveries.
