Pair-trade opportunities exist between LLYVA and LLYVK as the spread oscillates. Most importantly, Liberty Media's history shows that dual-class structures eventually collapse into single-class (FWONA/FWONK collapse precedent) — when that happens, LLYVK typically trades up to the LLYVA price. Buybacks at material NAV discount, possible eventual roll-up into LYV directly, and the seasoning of forced selling all argue for a 12-18 month re-rating.
