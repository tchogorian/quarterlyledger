### ANALYST SUMMARY
BCAT is a $1.5B BlackRock multi-asset closed-end fund (equity + credit + opportunistic) structured as a "term trust" — meaning it has a contractual dissolution date around 2032 at which point shareholders receive NAV. Saba Capital filed Schedule 13D/A May 19, 2025 disclosing 5,352,475 shares = 5.11% stake at ~$78.4M cost. Current price $14.80 vs. NAV $14.56 (research file shows -1.6% discount; BlackRock's Q4 2025 commentary says 2025 average discount was -4.8%, below the -10% Discount Management Program threshold so no 2025 tender). Per Q4 commentary: "Trust did not issue a tender offer in 2025 as its average discount was -4.8%."

Bear case: the discount has already narrowed dramatically — much of the easy alpha is captured. Saba sub-5% means no immediate proxy fight catalyst; multi-asset CEFs face compressed credit spreads in 2026 and any rate volatility hits NAV directly. The DMP threshold sits at 10% — wide gaps would have to reopen for a forced tender, which competing supply/demand from BlackRock support buying may prevent. If markets sell off, NAV drops faster than discount tightens, hurting absolute returns.

Bull case: Saba's BlackRock track record is undefeated — BIGZ/BMEZ-style 50% tenders at 99.5% of NAV. Even without a tender, the 2032 hard maturity provides pull-to-NAV with ~6 years of duration. Saba can escalate to trustee nominations at the 2026 annual meeting. Cushion: shareholders have a contractual right to NAV at term — every year that passes pulls market price toward NAV at ~mathematical certainty. BlackRock often pre-empts proxy fights with negotiated tenders.

### WHAT A SCREEN CAN'T SEE
The term-trust prospectus contains a board option to extend dissolution beyond 2032 by shareholder vote — Saba's 5.11% stake plus likely allied CEF activists creates a blocking position against extension. Per BlackRock filings: the Board "may vote to conduct a tender offer within twelve months preceding the Dissolution Date... at NAV per common share." Saba doesn't need to win a proxy fight to capture NAV — they just need to vote against any extension. This effectively converts BCAT into a 6-year zero-coupon bond on NAV with embedded equity upside.

### CATALYSTS (next 6-12 months)

- DMP measurement period (Jan–Sep 2026): if average discount >10%, triggers 5% tender at 98% NAV
- Possible Saba 13D/A escalation if discount widens during equity selloff
